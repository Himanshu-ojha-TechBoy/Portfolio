Overview

This is a simple AI game bot that plays Rock, Paper, Scissors with you and tracks your score. I built it using the 'Random' library and 'If-Else' statements. When you input 'rock', 'paper', or 'scissors', the bot compares your choice against seven built-in conditions to determine the winner and update the score.

I built this project entirely by myself, without using any AI tools.
